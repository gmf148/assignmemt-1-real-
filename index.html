Early Life

Aubrey Drake Graham was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto. After his parents divorced, Drake was primarily raised by his mother.

Beginnings in Music

After appearing on the television show Degrassi, Drake began to get into music. During his time on the show, he began to write and record. In 2006, he released his first mixtape, Room for Improvement.
